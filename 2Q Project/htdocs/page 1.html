    about 'the psych void'

    The Psych Void is a simple website dedicated to informing and educating you about necessary knowledge for mental health awareness. It includes surface information about common mental illnesses, must-knows in handling your own or others' mental maladies, and other information that may help!






    Disclaimer: I am not a mental health professional/specialist, so there may be inaccuracies in this list. Also, please avoid self-diagnosing. As much as possible, please consult a psychologist/psychiatrist if you feel like you have the symptoms.
